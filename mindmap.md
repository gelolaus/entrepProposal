# Online Marketplaces (by Seedly.sg)

## Carousell
- It's easy and requires no effort to list items.
- There are no seller fees but there are listing renewal fees.
- Buyers can pay using CarouPay or other third-party payment providers.
- The delivery services are arranged fully by the seller.

## Instagram Marketplace
- It's easy and requires no effort to add item listings.
- There are no seller fees.
- The payment services are handled by third-party payment providers.
- THe delivery services are fully arranged by the seller.

## Shopee
- It's easy but requires mid effort to list items.
- There are both transaction fees and services fees that sellers need to pay.
- They offer their own in-app payment service, ShopeePay.
- The delivery services are handled by logistic partners.

## Lazada
- Requires tedious and high effort work to list items.
- There are payment and shipping fees for sellers.
- They have their own in-app payment service.
- Logistics partners handle their delivery services.

# Rangya

## Listing of Items
- Sellers will only be allowed to sell high-stake collectibles to build the trust of buyers.
- Sellers will have to provide their product's legitimacy through verification processes.

## Seller Fees
- There are no seller fees.

## Payment Services
- The seller will be given the chance to choose their preferred method of payment.
- Meet, RangyaPay.

## Delivery Services
- The seller and the buyer can discuss on their own their preferred delivery service.
- The buyer can also choose their preferred logistic partner to handle their delivery service.
